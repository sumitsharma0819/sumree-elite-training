Phase 3 at 170 lb
What changes when you hit goal weight
Hitting 170 lb is the most dangerous moment of the yo-yo cycle. The protocol does not end — it evolves.
  • Keep exact exercise structure — same movements, same days
  • Raise calories by +150–200 kcal/day each week until maintenance
  • Raise protein to 175–190g/day for muscle-building phase
  • Add 1 extra set to biceps, triceps, lateral raises on Day 2 and Day 5
  • Keep Zone 2 at 1–2 sessions/week — do not eliminate cardio base
  • Continue all right-side asymmetry work — indefinitely
  • Continue full injury warm-up sequence — permanently
